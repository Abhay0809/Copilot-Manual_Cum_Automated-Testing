# Product Module Test Scenarios

1. Verify product listing page loads with product cards.
2. Verify each product card displays name, image, and price.
3. Verify selecting a product opens correct product details page.
4. Verify product details page displays consistent data with listing.
5. Verify search returns matching products for valid keyword.
6. Verify search with no matching keyword shows proper no-results behavior.
7. Verify search is case-insensitive for typical keywords.
8. Verify product listing pagination/scroll behavior works (if applicable).
9. Verify category filter updates product results correctly (if available).
10. Verify brand filter updates product results correctly (if available).
11. Verify add-to-cart from listing works and confirms action.
12. Verify add-to-cart from product details works and updates cart count.
