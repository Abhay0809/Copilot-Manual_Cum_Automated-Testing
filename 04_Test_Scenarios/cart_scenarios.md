# Cart Test Scenarios

1. Verify adding a single product updates cart item count.
2. Verify adding multiple different products displays all items in cart.
3. Verify adding same product twice updates quantity as designed.
4. Verify cart line total equals unit price multiplied by quantity.
5. Verify grand total equals sum of all line totals.
6. Verify removing a product updates cart totals correctly.
7. Verify updating quantity recalculates totals immediately.
8. Verify cart persists while navigating between pages in same session.
9. Verify cart page handles empty cart state with clear message.
10. Verify checkout button behavior from cart page is correct.
11. Verify cart displays correct product names/prices/images.
12. Verify invalid quantity input is blocked or corrected safely.
