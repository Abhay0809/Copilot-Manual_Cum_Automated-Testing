# Automation Exercise Website Overview

## Purpose

Automation Exercise (https://automationexercise.com/) is a demo e-commerce platform designed for QA practice and test automation learning. It simulates a real online shopping experience, including customer account creation, product discovery, cart handling, checkout flow, and customer support interactions.

## Business Context

- Domain: B2C online retail
- User goal: Discover products, add items to cart, and place orders
- Business goal: Provide a reliable and user-friendly shopping journey that supports conversions and customer retention

## Major Features

- User account management:
  - Signup / account creation
  - Login / logout
  - Account deletion
- Catalog and shopping:
  - Product listing
  - Product details view
  - Search products
  - Category and brand filtering (where available)
- Cart and order:
  - Add to cart from listing/details
  - Quantity update and item removal
  - Checkout and payment flow (simulated)
- Customer engagement:
  - Contact Us form
  - Newsletter subscription

## Quality Expectations

- Functional accuracy for end-to-end purchase flow
- Correct pricing and cart calculations
- Secure and robust authentication behavior
- Usable and responsive UI across common devices
- Clear error handling and validation messages

## Practice Value for QA

This website is suitable for manual QA portfolio work because it contains multiple integrated modules, positive and negative paths, and critical transactional workflows that can be covered through test planning, scenario design, traceability, and defect reporting.
