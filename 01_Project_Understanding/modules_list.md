# Modules Available for Testing

## Core Functional Modules

1. Signup
2. Login
3. Product Listing
4. Product Details
5. Search
6. Cart
7. Checkout
8. Contact Us
9. Newsletter Subscription

## Supporting Areas (Optional Extended Scope)

1. Logout
2. Account Deletion
3. Category navigation
4. Brand filtering
5. Recommended items section

## Module Prioritization (Initial)

- High: Signup, Login, Cart, Checkout
- Medium: Product Listing, Product Details, Search
- Low to Medium: Contact Us, Newsletter Subscription

## Dependencies

- Login depends on successful account creation.
- Checkout depends on cart state and user authentication.
- Cart behaviors depend on product availability and pricing data from listing/details pages.
